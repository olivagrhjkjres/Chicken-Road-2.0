Chicken Road 2.0

Play and Win in new Chicken Road

“Chicken Road 2.0” redefines the classic genre of road games with a brain workout! Memorize a grid of 2x2, 3x3 or 4x4 chicken tiles before they flip over. Every second counts – tap to select looped images and restore the sequence in time! To conquer levels, unlock clues in daily rewards or buy for points won, such as +5s memorization time or instant card reveals. Earn points and prove your memorization skills in this fast-paced, intuitive game!
